# \## 📊 What is This?

# 

# This project generates a \*\*complete, enterprise-grade Excel workbook\*\* for FMCG sales and distribution organizations. It creates a fully formula-driven dashboard with \*\*126 live KPIs\*\* across 6 business functions.

# 

# \*\*Perfect for:\*\* CEOs, Board of Directors, Sales Directors, CFOs, Supply Chain Managers, and Management Consultants.

# 

# \---

# 

# \## ✨ Key Features

# 

# | Feature | Description |

# |---------|-------------|

# | \*\*126 Live KPIs\*\* | Sales, Customer, Inventory, Distribution, Finance, Advanced |

# | \*\*Executive Dashboard\*\* | 15+ core KPIs with status indicators (✅ ⚠️ 🔴) |

# | \*\*Conditional Formatting\*\* | Auto-highlight good/bad performance |

# | \*\*Interactive Charts\*\* | Line, Pie, and Bar charts for executive reporting |

# | \*\*Pivot Analysis\*\* | Pre-built pivot tables for custom analysis |

# | \*\*VBA Automation\*\* | Built-in macros for refresh and PDF export |

# | \*\*9 Data Tables\*\* | 5,000+ rows of realistic FMCG data |

# | \*\*Bilingual\*\* | Persian and English naming for all KPIs |

# 

# \---

# 

# \## 📁 Workbook Structure

# 

# | Sheet | Content |

# |-------|---------|

# | `Data\_Sales` | 5,000 transactions with product, channel, and financial fields |

# | `Data\_Customers` | 200 customer records with segmentation |

# | `Data\_Products` | 50 SKU master records with cost and pricing |

# | `Data\_Inventory` | Warehouse stock with ABC/XYZ classification |

# | `Data\_Visits` | 3,000 sales visit records with compliance tracking |

# | `Data\_External` | 12 months of market and competitor data |

# | `Data\_AR\_Aging` | 400 AR invoice records |

# | `Data\_Targets` | Monthly sales rep targets |

# | `Assumptions` | All business parameters in one place |

# | `KPI\_\*` (6 sheets) | 126 live formula-based KPIs |

# | `Dashboard` | Executive summary with status indicators |

# | `Pivot\_Analysis` | Ready-to-use pivot tables |

# | `VBA\_Guide` | Macro instructions for automation |

# \## 📈 126 KPIs Across 6 Business Functions

# 

# \### Sales \& Marketing (31 KPIs)

# Net Revenue, Gross Margin, Sales Growth, Revenue per Customer, Revenue per Rep, Discount Rate, Return Rate, EBITDA, Net Profit, and more.

# 

# \### Customer Analytics (20 KPIs)

# Retention Rate, Churn Rate, CLV, CAC, NPS, CSAT, RFM Analysis, Customer Coverage, and more.

# 

# \### Inventory Management (12 KPIs)

# Turnover, Stockout Rate, Overstock Rate, Fill Rate, Days to Expiry, ABC/XYZ Analysis, and more.

# 

# \### Distribution \& Route (16 KPIs)

# Numeric Distribution, Weighted Distribution, Strike Rate, On-Time Delivery, Perfect Order Rate, Planogram Compliance, and more.

# 

# \### Financial Performance (17 KPIs)

# Net Margin, ROI, ROE, ROCE, EVA, DSO, DPO, Cash Conversion Cycle, Working Capital, and more.

# 

# \### Advanced \& Enterprise (30 KPIs)

# Market Share, Forecast Accuracy, Price Elasticity, Business Health Score, Sustainability Score, and more.
